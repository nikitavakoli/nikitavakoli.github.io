Welcome!

I'm a PhD student studying Biomedical Enginnering at the University Southern California Viterbi School of Engineering in Biomedical Engineering. I completed my M.S. in 2021 also at USC, and my B.A. from the University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA).

I work in the Computational Systems Biology Laboratory (CSBL) and am passionate about the subfield of Mathematical Oncology, which is the unification of applied mathematics and computational frameworks to gain insights into cancer mechanisms, progression, and targeted patient therapy. My collaborative project focuses on utilizing a Systems Biology approach to elucidate tumor-stromal metabolic crosstalk that occurs between cancer-associated fibroblasts and cancer cells in the tumor microenvironment (NIH U01 grant).
